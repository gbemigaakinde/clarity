FAQ

Frequently asked questions

How do I get started?

Create a free account, browse our course catalog, and enroll in any course that interests you. You'll get instant access to start learning right away.

Do I get lifetime access to courses?

Yes. Once you enroll, you have permanent access to all course materials, including any future updates the instructor makes.

Can I learn at my own pace?

Absolutely. All courses are self-paced. Learn on your schedule, pause and resume anytime, and revisit lessons as many times as you need.

Do I receive a certificate upon completion?

Yes. Completing a course earns you a certificate you can share on LinkedIn and add to your resume.

What if I'm not satisfied?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied for any reason, contact our support team for a full refund.
