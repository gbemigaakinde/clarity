Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

How do I get started?

Simply create a free account, browse our course catalog, and enroll in any course that interests you. You'll get instant access to start learning right away.

Do I get lifetime access to courses?

Yes! Once you enroll in a course, you have lifetime access to all course materials, including future updates.

Are there any prerequisites?

Prerequisites vary by course. Each course page clearly lists any required knowledge or skills. Many courses are beginner-friendly and require no prior experience.

Can I learn at my own pace?

Absolutely! All courses are self-paced. Learn on your schedule, pause and resume anytime, and revisit lessons as many times as you need.

Do I receive a certificate upon completion?

Yes! Upon completing a course, you'll receive a certificate of completion that you can share on LinkedIn and add to your resume.

What if I'm not satisfied with a course?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all courses. If you're not satisfied, contact our support team for a full refund.
